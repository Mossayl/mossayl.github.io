Who

建模者，时代乐子人。想要钱，但对很多钱没什么欲望。对做正确的事有执念。

A model-builder. A spectator of the era having fun. Wants money, but not obsessed with a lot of it. Obsessed with doing the right thing.

Origin

2024 年，第一次用 Kimi。那一刻意识到教育会被彻底颠覆——AI 会给每个有想法和改变欲望的人一个相对公平的机会。

In 2024, the first time using Kimi. That moment it became clear: education will be fundamentally disrupted. AI gives everyone with ideas and the desire to change a relatively fair shot.

不是所有人都有 Claude Code 的额度，也不是所有人都有跨越惰性门槛的想法。但那些有的人，值得更好的工具。

Not everyone has a Claude Code subscription. Not everyone has the will to cross the inertia threshold. But those who do deserve better tools.

What I Want to Build

一个 AI 主动阅读器：根据你的知识水平和好奇心，给出最好的提问方式，和适配你水平的回答。AI 应该主动，应该自我进化。

An AI-native reader that proactively shapes questions for you — based on what you know, what you're curious about — and answers at your level. AI should be proactive. AI should self-evolve.

更远一点：AI 协助人完成自我探索。在社会规训、职业经验、传统教育的噪声下，很少有人能确认和发挥自己真正的想法。

Further out: AI-assisted self-discovery. Under the noise of social conditioning, career conventions, and traditional education, few people ever confirm and act on what they truly think.

How I Think

擅长从碎片信息中串联建模，做出阶段性的犀利判断。Token 经济学、Agent 发展路径、产品形态预测——这些是我的舒适区。

Good at connecting fragments into models and making sharp, staged predictions. Token economics, agent evolution, product form — my comfort zone.

但阶段性的判断有明显局限。世界变量太多。Manus 被收购，智谱活了下来，OpenClaw 横空出世——碎片拼凑的未来，永远是不完整的。

But staged predictions have clear limits. Too many variables. Manus got acquired. Zhipu survived. OpenClaw appeared out of nowhere. A future assembled from fragments is always incomplete.

Limits

会为错误反复反刍。阶段性预测往往导致悲观和极端情绪。犀利的判断带来正确感和急迫感，但世界不按阶段性的认知运转。

Ruminates over mistakes. Staged predictions often lead to pessimism and emotional extremes. Sharp judgments bring a sense of being right and a sense of urgency — but the world doesn't run on staged cognition.

25 岁，在 AI 的协助下理解了大概 60% 的自己。剩下 40% 是变动和迭代的。

At 25, understood roughly 60% of myself with AI's help. The remaining 40% is fluid and iterative.

Belief

悲观永远正确。乐观才有未来。

Pessimism is always right. Optimism is the only way forward.

对个人命运的预测是极难的。唯一可以相信的，是理性乐观的科技未来，并且积极推动它发生。

Predicting personal fate is nearly impossible. The only thing worth believing in is a rationally optimistic technological future — and actively pushing it to happen.

一个聪明、迭代、不断学习的年轻人，只要有本能的学习欲望驱使，虽然不一定很好，但一定不坏。

A smart, iterative, ever-learning young person, driven by an instinctive desire to learn — may not end up great, but certainly won't end up bad.
